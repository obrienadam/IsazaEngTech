Custom Engineering Software

The complexity of modern engineering projects often warrants the design of highly specialized tools. We offer software solutions for a number of engineering applications, built in house by our highly trained team of engineers with academic and industrial backgrounds. We also offer support and development services, and can work with you to build tools to aid in the design and maintenance of engineering systems.

Poiseulle - Node Network Flow Optimization

Our flagship software product, Poiseulle, allows the user to model complex fluid systems with an easy-to-use graphical interface. The solver itself is capable of solving flows through arbitrarily connected systems. It has a variety of built-in models for fluid mechanics, and can easily be extended to accommodate customized models. Our team is available to consult with users to develop extended models that are tailored to each client's individual requirements.

Currently, Poiseulle is suitable for:

  • Design of pneumatic manufacturing systems
  • HVAC design and optimization
  • Dust control systems
  • Hydraulic systems

Additionally, Poiseulle contains a built-in optimizer than can assist in the sizing of fans, pumps, valves etc. This ability to quickly construct models of fluid systems and perform optimizations greatly reduces the amount of time needed to bring new plants/systems online; and takes a great deal of uncertainty out of the design process.
